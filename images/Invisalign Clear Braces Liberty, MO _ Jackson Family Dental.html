Straighter teeth in as little as 6 months

Lots of people have crooked teeth — about 300 million! Thankfully, there’s an easy solution. At Jackson Family Dental, we offer Invisalign as our preferred tooth-straightening system for people of all ages.

Invisalign is a great option for those wanting to avoid the hassle, discomfort, and time commitment of traditional metal braces. This highly effective treatment uses a series of clear, custom-made aligners and offers beautiful results.

Be wary of trendy mail-order teeth straightening kits and their big marketing budgets. Something as serious as tooth alignment should always be directed and supervised by a dentist. Questions? We offer open and honest answers to help you make the best decision.

Fast facts about Invisalign

$

Who is a candidate for Invisalign?

The majority of people can benefit from this cosmetic treatment. The answer often comes down to your ability and willingness to follow the treatment plan. Ask yourself, “Can I commit to wearing my aligners for 22 hours every day?”

$

Can a teenager get Invisalign?

Invisalign easily treats most cases of underbite, overbite, overcrowding, and gaps — all common issues for teens. Some teens have a hard time wearing their aligners for the recommended 22 hours a day. If you think this will be the case for your teenager, traditional braces may be the better choice. 

$

How much does Invisalign cost?

Invisalign tends to be a similar cost to traditional braces, ranging from $4,000-$6,000 depending on the complexity of your situation. Many insurances cover some Invisalign treatment costs. As a fee-for-service office, we also offer payment plans and financing options to help you budget for care.

What you get with Invisalign at Jackson Family Dental

  • We use 3D imaging to visualize and precisely plan your results
  • You’ll receive custom-made aligners that will gradually move your teeth into proper position
  • Each set of aligners must be worn for 22 hours a day
  • You’ll switch to a new set of aligners every few weeks on your own
  • Visit us every 5 weeks or so and we’ll track your progress
  • The average Invisalign treatment takes 12-18 months (but you’ll start to see results in just a few weeks!)

We love helping people straighten their teeth through Invisalign — it’s one of our favorite treatments to direct and support. We’ll work with you one-on-one to make sure your treatment is going according to plan and be available to answer questions whenever you need.
