Tooth Whitening (Bleaching)

If your teeth aren't as white as you'd like, tooth whitening or bleaching can help you out. It's an easy and successful way to whiten the color of your teeth. As we age, our teeth naturally turn a bit yellow, and things like tea, coffee, and colas can speed up this process. Smoking and other things that stain can make your teeth darker, too. The whitening process removes away the staining and gradually brightens your teeth. We can help figure out what's best for your teeth and gums. Bleaching is a common and liked way to make teeth whiter. Some folks do it to get rid of stains, while others just want a brighter smile.

Types of bleaching

Office Bleach

Your dentist can make your teeth whiter right in their office.
After examining the tooth, dentist will start by cleaning the tooth. He will remove the film on your enamel coated due to the food you eat or due to other substances. The whitening process begins after this. The entire process takes around 30-90 mins depending upon the severity of the stains on teeth.

Home Bleach

If you prefer doing it at home, we have a system for that too. During your first visit, we take a mold of your upper and lower teeth. Then, we create a tray that fits your gums perfectly. When you come back for your second appointment, you try on this custom tray. If needed, we make it even more comfy for you. We give you the whitening solution and tell you how to use it at home. Easy!
