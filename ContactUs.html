PRICING

How much does emergency dental care cost?

Our pricing for emergency visits is determined by the required treatment. If you visit during our regular business hours, we can provide you with an estimate of your potential out-of-pocket expenses based on your insurance plan. Please note that this estimate is not a definitive final cost and may vary. Unfortunately, we are unable to offer cost estimates for emergency appointments scheduled after regular business hours.

Payment Options

While we are part of the Delta Premier network, it's important to note that our practice operates on a fee-for-service basis. For services provided by other insurance providers, our office will handle the billing. We also accept payments in the form of cash, checks, and credit cards for your convenience.
